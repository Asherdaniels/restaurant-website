Our story

A coastal soul, downtown

How a six-seat counter and one wood oven grew into a neighborhood table.

Est. 2014

Simply, generously, in season.

Asher's Bites began as a tiny six-seat counter and a single wood oven. A decade later we still cook the way we started — simply, generously, and with respect for the seasons.

Chef Lena Moreau draws on summers spent along the Mediterranean, translating sun-drenched markets into plates meant for sharing. Pull up a chair, order a little of everything, and stay a while.

— Lena Moreau, Chef & Owner
