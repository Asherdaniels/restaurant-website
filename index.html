Our story

A neighborhood table with a coastal soul.

Saffron Table began as a tiny six-seat counter and a single wood oven. A decade later we still cook the way we started — simply, generously, and with respect for the seasons.

Chef Lena Moreau draws on summers spent along the Mediterranean, translating sun-drenched markets into plates meant for sharing. Pull up a chair, order a little of everything, and stay a while.

— Lena Moreau, Chef & Owner
